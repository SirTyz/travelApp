Explore Reykjavík

Despite our relatively short history, modern Reykjavík is home to an impressive collection of interesting attractions and places of historic significance.

Otherworldly

Whilst the downtown area of Reykjavík has the highest concentration of cultural institutions, it also has an extraordinary number of tour operators offering everything from volcano tours to diving in the otherworldly waters of Silfra.

Nature, Parks & Gardens

Step off the pavements and explore paradise - all within Reykjavík city limits! Taking a break from culture and civilization is a must for residents of Reykjavík and with such a healthy number of beautiful parks and gardens, an area of green respite is never too far away.

Museums & History

Enter our past with on of the dozens of historical museums and sites in and around the Reykjavík area showing the everyday life of people from years passed. Learn about the everyday person and the Vikings that settled this land long ago.
